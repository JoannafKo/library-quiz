About

de omni re scibili et quibusdam aliis

This website is a quiz and study guide in one. For both begginers and students studying Latin. This isn't to just provide individuals tools to study, this is a tool to educate those same individuals on the impact this dead language has on modern Western languages

Even if you are not a student, it is still fascinating to learn about the root words of English words, such as "cinis" to "ash" or "nubes" to "cloud." Greco-Roman culture did not just impact policies and norms, but language too
